Every morning before departure, the engineer should perform a quick walk-through
of the engine room and auxiliary systems.

## Key Items

- Oil levels on main engines and generator
- Coolant levels and visible leaks
- Belt condition and tension
- Bilge status and unusual smells
- Battery bank voltage and corrosion

## Notes

Any new abnormal vibration, smell, or sound should be logged and communicated
to the captain before getting underway.
